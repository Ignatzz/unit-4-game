Crystal Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number - if your score exceeds that random number, however, you lose!

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Whenever a new game starts, a new random number will be chosen, and the crystal wil have new random values as well.
